About Us
AmbiTeach was founded on the idea that a teacher should be able to learn how to adapt to their class quickly and effectively. Using a simple platform, teachers and students can now connect in an unprecedented way.

In the short run, teachers can see real-time parameters of the classroom.

In the long run, the teacher can track both his or her performance as well as the students performance using AmbiTeach's data analysis system.
